## VPN (Virtual Private Network)

A VPN creates a secure and encrypted connection between a user and the internet.

Goal:
Protect privacy and secures data transmission.

---

## IDS (Intrusion Detection System)

An IDS monitors network or system activity and detects suspicious behavior or potential attacks.

Goal:
Identify security threats and generate alerts.

---

## Hashing

Hashing converts data into a fixed-length value called a hash.

Characteristics:
- One-way process
- Cannot be easily reversed
- Used for password storage

Examples:
- MD5
- SHA-256

---

## IP Address

An IP (Internet Protocol) Address is a unique identifier assigned to a device on a network.

Types:
- IPv4 (32 bit)
- IPv6 (128 bit)

Example:
- 192.168.1.1 (IPv4)
- 2001:0db8:85a3:0000:0000:8a2e:0370:7334 (IPv6)

Goal:
Identify and communicate with devices on a network.

---

## MAC Address

A MAC (Media Access Control) Address is a unique physical address assigned to a network interface card (NIC).

Example:
00:1A:2B:3C:4D:5E

Goal:
Identify devices within a local network.

---

## Cybersecurity Laws and Ethics

Cybersecurity laws define legal rules for using computers, networks, and digital systems.

Cybersecurity ethics refers to responsible and lawful behavior while using cybersecurity knowledge.

Examples:
- Respect privacy
- Obtain authorization before testing systems
- Follow legal guidelines
